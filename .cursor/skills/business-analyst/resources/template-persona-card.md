# Persona Card Template

Use this template to define a user persona. A persona is not a job title; it is a set of behaviors, needs, and constraints.

## [Persona Name]
*The [Role/Archetype Descriptor]*

### Who are they?
- **Background**: [Brief context on their experience and professional setting]
- **Key Responsibilities**: [What are they actually trying to achieve in their day-to-day?]
- **Relationship to the System**: [How do they interact with this specific application?]

### What do they need?
- **Primary Goals**: [The outcomes they value most]
- **Information Needs**: [What data do they need to see to make decisions?]
- **Desired Experience**: [Should it be fast? Should it be foolproof? Should it be highly flexible?]

### What are their constraints?
- **Technical Literacy**: [Are they power users or infrequent users?]
- **Time/Focus**: [Do they have 30 minutes of deep focus or 30 seconds between other tasks?]
- **Environmental Constraints**: [Are they on mobile? In a noisy office? Behind a restrictive firewall?]

### Their "So What?"
- **Success looks like**: [A concrete scenario where they feel the system worked for them]
- **Frustration looks like**: [A specific friction point that would cause them to abandon the task]

---

*Usage: Create a copy of this for each distinct stakeholder archetype identified during business analysis discovery.*
